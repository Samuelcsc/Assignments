About Me

I am Adeola, a web developer from Oyo state but currently based in Lagos. I got my bachelors degree in Computer Science from Funaab. I enjoy watching movies, music, gaming, swimming and meeting new people. I only enjoy travelling when it is by air and I am open to new experiences. Asides coding, I also have some knowlegde in Figma and I have some personal social media pages that I manage.

As a young boy, I have always been fascinated with technology and my quest to know more increased on a daily basis. I also got to play a lot of computer games too, which increased my liking for computers...thanks to my elder brother. That was in the days when desktop computers were common. He was also a computer science student and he usually brings his PC home during the holidays.

Apart from the fact that tech gives you some kind of flexibility that many jobs will never give you, it also pays well. Coupled with my love for computers, this made me choose tech as a career. I hope to be a fullstack developer soon. So help me God.
